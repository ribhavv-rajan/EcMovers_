How Much Do Movers Cost
in Windsor, Ontario?

One of the first questions people ask when planning a move is: how much is this going to cost? In Windsor, Ontario, moving costs vary considerably based on the size of your home, how far you're going, when you're moving, and what special items you have. Here's a transparent breakdown.

Local Moving Rates in Windsor

Most local moves in Windsor are priced hourly. Typical rates as of 2025:

  • 2 movers + truck: $120–$160/hour
  • 3 movers + truck: $160–$210/hour
  • 4 movers + truck: $200–$260/hour

Most companies have a minimum charge of 2–3 hours. Travel time (from the mover's depot to your home and back) may also be billed.

How Long Does Each Move Type Take?

  • 1-bedroom apartment: 2–4 hours
  • 2-bedroom apartment: 3–5 hours
  • 3-bedroom house: 5–8 hours
  • 4-bedroom house: 7–10+ hours

These are estimates — stairs, elevators, long carries, and volume all affect actual time.

💡 The fastest way to reduce your moving cost is to declutter before the move and have everything packed and ready when the crew arrives.

What Adds to Your Moving Quote

  • Specialty items — pianos, hot tubs, pool tables, and safes all add to the cost
  • Stairs and no elevator — additional time means additional cost
  • Long carry distance — if the truck can't park close to the entrance
  • Packing services — professional packing adds cost but saves time and stress
  • Storage — if your new home isn't ready on the same day
  • Date and timing — summer weekends cost more than mid-week winter moves

Long-Distance Moving Costs from Windsor

Long-distance moves are priced by weight and distance, not by the hour. A Windsor-to-Toronto move for a two-bedroom apartment typically runs $1,500–$3,000. A cross-country move can range from $5,000 to $15,000+ depending on volume.

Getting an Accurate Quote

The best quotes are based on an in-person or virtual walkthrough — not a rough estimate over the phone. Provide your mover with a full list of rooms, specialty items, and access details for both locations to get a number you can rely on.
