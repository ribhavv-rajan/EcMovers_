Condo Moving Tips in Windsor:
Elevators, Booking Rules, and Timing

Moving into or out of a condo in Windsor involves a layer of coordination that house moves don't — you're sharing infrastructure with dozens or hundreds of other residents, and building management has strict rules about how and when moves happen. Here's how to navigate it all.

Step 1: Contact Building Management Early

As soon as you have a confirmed move date, contact your condo's property manager. Most Windsor condo buildings require:

  • Advance notice of your move (typically 48–72 hours minimum, often 1–2 weeks)
  • A booking for the freight elevator with a specific time window (often 2–4 hours)
  • A damage deposit or insurance certificate from your moving company
  • Proof of move-in/move-out permission from the unit owner (for tenants)

💡 Elevator windows fill up fast in high-rise buildings — especially on weekends and at month-end. Book as early as possible and get written confirmation.

Working Within the Elevator Window

Most condo elevator bookings in Windsor give you a 2–4 hour window. To make the most of this time:

  • Have movers arrive 15–20 minutes before the window opens to prep and wrap
  • Pre-stage boxes and small items near the elevator so loading starts immediately
  • Have large furniture disassembled in advance — don't waste window time on this
  • Confirm the window length with your mover so they staff the right crew size

Hallway and Common Area Protection

Many Windsor condo buildings require movers to install floor runners in hallways and elevator pads in the freight elevator. Confirm with building management whether this is required and whether they supply the pads or whether your mover must bring them.

Moving Day Timing Tips

  • Mid-week moves avoid peak elevator competition with other residents
  • Morning windows (8–12 am) tend to be less congested than afternoon
  • Month-end (last three days of the month) is the busiest time in condo buildings — avoid if possible
  • If you can't avoid month-end, book your elevator window weeks in advance
