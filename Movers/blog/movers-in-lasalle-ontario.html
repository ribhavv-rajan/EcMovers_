About Moving in LaSalle

LaSalle is a predominantly residential community bordering Windsor to the west. Its housing stock is largely composed of newer detached and semi-detached homes, townhouses, and some condominium developments — many of which are in growing subdivisions with wide streets and easy truck access. This makes LaSalle moves generally more straightforward than densely urban ones.

Tips for a Smooth LaSalle Move

  • If you're in a newer subdivision, confirm your address is correctly listed — GPS can sometimes route incorrectly to new streets
  • If moving into a LaSalle townhouse, measure stair width and ceiling height for large furniture
  • Book summer moves (May–August) at least 4 weeks in advance — LaSalle is busy during peak season
  • Let your mover know if your driveway is concrete or interlocking stone — some surfaces require ramp boards to protect them
