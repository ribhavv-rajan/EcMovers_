Windsor to London Movers:
Long-Distance Moving Tips

London, Ontario sits about 190 km northeast of Windsor — roughly a two-hour drive. It's one of the most common long-distance moves from Windsor, particularly for students heading to Western University, families relocating for work, and retirees downsizing. Here's what you need to know to plan it well.

How Windsor to London Moves Are Priced

At 190 km, a Windsor-to-London move is typically priced as a mid-range long-distance move:

  • Flat rate: Common for this distance — one price covers everything
  • Weight-based: Some companies use shipment weight, more common at greater distances

Estimated costs (2025) for a Windsor-to-London move:

  • 1-bedroom apartment: $1,200–$2,000
  • 2-bedroom home: $1,800–$3,000
  • 3-bedroom home: $2,500–$4,500+

These are estimates — specialty items, access challenges, and packing services all affect the final number.

💡 For a Windsor-to-London move, always get a written, binding flat-rate quote. Non-binding quotes based on estimates can change significantly at the end of the move.

Packing for a Windsor to London Move

Items travel further and endure more vibration on a two-hour highway move than a local one. Pack to a higher standard:

  • Use double-wall boxes for dishes, glassware, and fragile items
  • Fill every box completely — nothing should shift inside
  • Wrap all fragile items individually
  • Use quality tape — cheap tape fails under load and transit vibration
  • Label every box clearly on the top and all four sides

What to Confirm with Your Mover

  • Is the quote binding or non-binding?
  • Is fuel and travel included, or added separately?
  • Will it be a dedicated truck or a shared load?
  • What is the delivery window — same day or multi-day?
  • What is the claims process if something is damaged?

Moving Specialty Items Windsor to London

Pianos, hot tubs, and pool tables can be moved on a Windsor-to-London route — but declare them when booking. Confirm the mover brings specialist equipment for the trip, not just standard furniture blankets.
