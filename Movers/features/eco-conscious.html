Our Green Commitment

A Greener Move
Is a Better Move.

The average home move generates a surprising amount of waste — cardboard boxes that get used once, packing materials that end up in landfills, and fuel burned on poorly-planned routes. We're working to change that.

EC Movers offers reusable plastic tote rentals as an eco-friendly alternative to single-use cardboard. We also plan every move to optimise fuel use — fewer trips, more efficient routes, less emissions.

  • Reusable plastic tote rentals — no cardboard waste
  • Fuel-efficient route planning on every job
  • Responsible disposal of packing materials post-move
  • Ongoing effort to reduce our fleet's environmental impact
