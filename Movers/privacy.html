Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1st, 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Pickup and delivery addresses
  • Moving dates and service details
  • Billing, deposit, and payment information
  • Communication records (calls, emails, forms, social media)
  • Website data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, cookies)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide quotes and moving services
  • Schedule and manage bookings
  • Process payments and deposits
  • Communicate regarding your move
  • Improve our website and services
  • Maintain records and meet legal obligations

3. Consent

By contacting us, submitting forms, or booking services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Our staff and subcontractors involved in your move

  • Insurance providers (if needed)
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required

5. Payments & Deposits

Deposits and payments may be processed through secure third-party providers or approved methods such as e-transfer, card, cash, or invoice.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to improve website performance and user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for legal, business, and operational purposes.

9. Access & Corrections

You may request access to or correction of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
