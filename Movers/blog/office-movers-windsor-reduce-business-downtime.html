Office Movers in Windsor:
How to Reduce Business Downtime During a Move

An office move that's poorly planned can cost your business days of lost productivity. The good news is that with the right approach — and the right movers — most Windsor businesses can complete a relocation in a single weekend and open in their new space on Monday morning. Here's how to make that happen.

Start Planning Earlier Than You Think You Need To

For most office moves in Windsor, planning should begin six to twelve weeks before the move date — earlier for larger spaces. Key early decisions include:

  • Choosing your moving date (off-peak for business = Friday evening or weekend)
  • Notifying employees and assigning departmental coordinators
  • Designing the layout of your new space before moving day
  • Auditing your current IT setup to plan the new one
  • Confirming lease start dates and access to the new space

The Power of Colour-Coded Labelling

Every box and piece of furniture should be labelled clearly with its destination department or zone in the new office. Use coloured tape or stickers — a different colour per department. Post a floor plan at the entrance of the new space. Movers can then place items directly in the right area without stopping to ask.

💡 Invest in a printed floor plan for moving day — one copy for the movers at the truck, one posted at the new office entrance. It saves significant time at the destination.

IT and Electronics: Your Biggest Priority

Computers, servers, and network equipment are the lifeblood of most businesses. Plan the IT move carefully:

  • Back up all data before the move — non-negotiable
  • Have your IT team or provider set up the new space before furniture arrives
  • Label all cables before disconnecting
  • Use anti-static bags for sensitive components
  • Transport monitors and servers in original packaging where possible

Communicate with Your Team

Employees who know what to expect are employees who can help. Give everyone clear instructions on what to pack personally (desk items, personal belongings), what to leave for the movers, and what the new space will look like.
