How to Choose a Moving Company:
Avoid Mistakes & Hire the Right Movers

Choosing the wrong moving company can turn your move into a nightmare — hidden fees, damaged items, or last-minute cancellations. Here's exactly how to hire a reliable moving company in Windsor.

1. Check Reviews & Reputation

Start with Google reviews. Look for consistent ratings, not just one or two reviews. A reliable mover should have a strong reputation for professionalism and care.

💡 Tip: Don’t just look at stars — read the reviews to see how companies handle problems.

2. Verify Insurance & Licensing

Always ask if the company is insured. Professional movers protect your belongings and take responsibility for damages.

3. Ask About Pricing Structure

Good movers are transparent. Avoid companies that give vague estimates or change prices last minute.

  • Hourly vs flat rate
  • Travel time charges
  • Extra fees (stairs, heavy items)

4. Look for Specialized Experience

If you have items like pianos, hot tubs, or pool tables, choose movers with specific experience and equipment.

5. Ask These Questions Before Booking

  • How many movers will be assigned?
  • What equipment do you bring?
  • Do you provide packing services?
  • What happens if something gets damaged?

6. Book Early

Most professional movers recommend booking 4–6 weeks in advance, especially during busy seasons.

🚛 Hiring professionals saves time, reduces stress, and prevents costly damage.
