How to Plan a Commercial Move
in Windsor-Essex County

A commercial move requires a fundamentally different approach than a residential one. You're coordinating people, equipment, operations, and deadlines — often while trying to keep your business running. Here's how to plan a commercial move in Windsor-Essex from start to finish.

Phase 1: Planning (8–12 Weeks Out)

  • Confirm your new lease or purchase date and access conditions
  • Choose a moving company experienced in commercial relocations
  • Appoint an internal move coordinator — one person who owns the entire process
  • Create an inventory of everything that's moving, including dimensions of large items
  • Design the layout of your new space before moving day — know where everything goes
  • Identify items to discard or donate rather than move

Phase 2: Preparation (4–6 Weeks Out)

  • Develop a room-by-room labelling system (colour per department or zone)
  • Order packing materials and distribute to departments
  • Schedule IT disconnection and reconnection with your provider
  • Notify clients, vendors, and service providers of your upcoming address change
  • Confirm utility activation at the new location
  • Arrange parking permits or building access for moving trucks at both sites

💡 The floor plan for the new space is your most important document on moving day. Print multiple copies — one for the moving crew at the truck, one posted at the entrance of the new space.

Phase 3: Pre-Move (1–2 Weeks Out)

  • Brief all staff on their responsibilities: what they pack personally vs. what movers handle
  • Label all equipment with destination zone and department
  • Back up all company data — no exceptions
  • Confirm move date, arrival time, and crew size with your moving company
  • Do a walkthrough of both locations and share notes with the move coordinator

Phase 4: Moving Day

  • Move coordinator present at both origin and destination throughout the day
  • Movers work from the posted floor plan — minimal direction needed if labelled properly
  • IT team begins reconnection as furniture and equipment is placed
  • Final walkthrough of old location before keys are returned

Phase 5: Post-Move

  • Test all equipment and connectivity before declaring the move complete
  • Update Google Business profile, website, and business cards
  • Schedule a post-move team debrief to capture lessons learned
