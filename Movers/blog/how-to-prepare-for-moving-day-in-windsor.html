How to Prepare for Moving Day
in Windsor

The difference between a smooth moving day and a stressful one often comes down to preparation. Even if your movers are experienced and efficient, there are things only you can do in advance to make sure the day runs on time and without surprises.

The Night Before Moving Day

  • Defrost your refrigerator completely and dry the interior
  • Disassemble beds, desks, and any large furniture that hasn't been done already
  • Pack your essentials bag — the one that travels with you, not in the truck
  • Set out phone chargers, IDs, and any documents you'll need on the day
  • Confirm your booking details with the moving company
  • Get a good night's sleep — moving day is physically and mentally demanding

Morning of Moving Day

  • Clear pathways from every room to the front door
  • Roll up rugs, move pets to a safe room or arrange care off-site
  • Prop open doors to speed up loading
  • Have cash or payment method ready for the movers
  • Put your essentials bag somewhere you won't confuse it with items going in the truck

💡 If you have children or pets, arrange for them to be elsewhere on moving day. It's safer, faster, and reduces everyone's stress.

When the Crew Arrives

  • Do a walkthrough with the lead mover before anything is loaded
  • Point out fragile, high-value, or unusual items
  • Share the floor plan for the new location if you have one
  • Show them where they can park the truck
  • Stay available throughout the move — don't disappear

Before You Leave Your Old Home

  • Walk through every room, closet, cupboard, and outdoor space
  • Check the attic, basement, crawl spaces, garden shed, and garage
  • Take meter readings for electricity and gas
  • Return keys to your landlord or real estate agent
  • Sign off on the moving inventory with your crew
  • Take photos of the vacated property for your records

At Your New Home

  • Test all locks before the crew leaves
  • Locate the fuse box, water shutoff, and gas shutoff
  • Direct movers to the correct rooms using your floor plan
  • Check off items against your inventory as they come off the truck
