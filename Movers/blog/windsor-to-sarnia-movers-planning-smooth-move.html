Windsor to Sarnia Movers:
Planning a Smooth Move

Windsor and Sarnia sit at opposite ends of Highway 402 in southwestern Ontario — roughly 100 km apart. It's a manageable one-day move, but the distance means it's structured differently from a local Windsor move. Here's how to plan it properly.

Pricing for Windsor to Sarnia Moves

At 100 km, a Windsor-to-Sarnia move is typically priced as a short-distance move rather than a full long-haul. Expect one of these structures:

  • Flat rate: One all-in price covering loading, transport, unloading, and travel
  • Hourly + mileage: Hourly rate for moving time plus a per-km or flat travel fee

Get a detailed quote that specifies exactly what's included — travel time, fuel, and any ferry or toll costs (there are none on this route, but it's good practice).

💡 The drive from Windsor to Sarnia via the 402 is 60–90 minutes depending on traffic and time of day — plan your start time so the crew isn't arriving at Sarnia in evening rush hour.

How to Prepare for a Windsor-Sarnia Move

  • Pack everything completely before the crew arrives — a long-distance same-day move has no time buffer
  • Label all boxes with destination room at the Sarnia address
  • Have someone confirmed at the Sarnia destination to receive the truck
  • Confirm parking and access at the Sarnia address — some downtown Sarnia streets have limited commercial vehicle access
  • Discuss specialty items (pianos, hot tubs) when booking — they require additional equipment and crew

Moving Specialty Items Windsor to Sarnia

Specialty items like pianos, hot tubs, and pool tables can absolutely be moved on a Windsor-to-Sarnia route — but they must be declared when booking. The right equipment needs to travel with the crew, and access at both ends needs to be pre-assessed.

After You Arrive in Sarnia

  • Update your Ontario driver's licence and health card at a ServiceOntario location
  • Register your vehicle with the new Sarnia address
  • Set up mail forwarding through Canada Post
  • Update CRA, your bank, employer, and insurance providers
