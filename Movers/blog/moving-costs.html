How Much Does a Local Move Cost
in Windsor, Ontario? (2025 Guide)

One of the most common questions we get is: "How much is this going to cost?" It's a fair question, and the frustrating reality is that most moving companies make it unnecessarily mysterious. Here's a completely transparent breakdown of what drives moving costs in Windsor-Essex in 2025.

How Windsor Movers Typically Charge

Hourly Rate (most common for local moves)

Most local moves in Windsor, LaSalle, Tecumseh, and surrounding areas are billed hourly, with a minimum of 2 hours. The clock typically starts when the truck leaves the depot and ends when it returns — so travel time is included.

EC Movers Ltd 2025 hourly rates:
• 16ft truck + 2 movers — from $120/hr
• 26ft truck + 2 movers — from $150/hr
• 26ft truck + 3 movers — from $190/hr

Flat-Rate (for long-distance moves)

For moves outside Windsor-Essex — Toronto, Ottawa, London, Calgary — we quote a flat rate based on your home size and distance. You pay one price, no matter how long the actual job takes.

What Affects Your Moving Cost

Home Size

  • Studio / 1-bedroom: 2–3 hours, $240–$450 (16ft truck + 2 movers)
  • 2-bedroom: 3–5 hours, $450–$750 (26ft truck + 2 movers)
  • 3-bedroom: 5–7 hours, $750–$1,050 (26ft truck + 2 movers)
  • 4+ bedroom: 6–9 hours, $900–$1,710 (26ft truck + 3 movers)

Distance

Local moves within Windsor-Essex are hourly. The further you move, the more travel time is included in your bill. For anything over 80 km from Windsor, we recommend a flat-rate quote.

Stairs and Elevator Access

Multiple flights of stairs or buildings without elevator access significantly slow down a move. Be upfront about access when getting a quote — it affects how many movers we assign.

Specialty Items

Pianos, pool tables, hot tubs, and safes require specialist equipment and time. These typically carry a flat surcharge in addition to the hourly rate — anywhere from $150 for an upright piano to $400+ for a grand piano or pool table.

Packing Services

If you'd like our crew to pack for you, this adds time (and therefore cost) to your move. Full packing for a 2-bedroom home typically adds 2–4 hours. The benefit: our packing is faster, safer, and fully insured.

Time of Year

Summer moves (May–August) are peak season. While our rates don't change, availability is limited and you may need to book 3–4 weeks ahead. Winter moves are easier to schedule and sometimes quicker due to weather-related efficiency.

How to Get the Most Accurate Quote

The best way to get an accurate price is to provide: your current address and destination, the number of bedrooms, any specialty items, floor level and elevator access at both ends, and whether you need packing.

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Movers who won't give you a written quote
  • Deposits over 20–25% of the total estimate
  • No proof of insurance when you ask
  • "Binding estimates" with suspiciously low prices
  • No physical address or contact number
