Pool Table Movers in Windsor:
Disassembly, Transport, and Setup

Pool tables look sturdy, but they're surprisingly delicate when it comes to moving. A full-size slate pool table can weigh 700–1,000 lbs, and the three-piece slate playing surface needs to be handled with care to avoid cracking. Moving a pool table without disassembling it properly can result in a table that never plays level again. Here's what the process looks like with professional pool table movers in Windsor.

Why You Can't Just Slide a Pool Table

Pool tables are built in place. The legs are bolted to the frame, the felt is stapled and glued to the slate, and the slate itself is heavy and brittle. Attempting to move the table as a unit — even with several people — risks cracking the slate, stripping leg bolts, and warping the frame.

The Professional Pool Table Moving Process

  1. Remove the pockets — unscrew or unclip and set aside
  2. Remove the rails — unbolt the rail sections and wrap them for protection
  3. Remove the felt — carefully cut or unstaple, depending on whether you're replacing it
  4. Lift the slate — slate sections are heavy (often 150–200 lbs each) and must be lifted with multiple people or mechanical assist
  5. Disassemble the frame and legs — unbolt the leg assemblies from the frame
  6. Transport carefully — slate travels flat, never on its edge
  7. Reassemble at destination — frame, slate, leveling, felt, rails, pockets

💡 Professional pool table movers will re-level the table at the destination — critical for accurate play. Always confirm leveling is included in your quote.

Felt Replacement: Should You Do It During the Move?

A pool table move is an ideal time to replace worn felt, since the slate is already exposed. If your felt is more than five years old or shows wear, ask your movers about the cost of re-felting during the relocation.

How Long Does a Pool Table Move Take?

Expect the full process — disassembly, transport, and setup — to take three to five hours depending on distance and home access. Multi-floor moves or tight stairwells add time.
