How Pool Table Movers in Windsor
Handle Slate Tables Safely

Slate pool tables are the gold standard for serious billiards — but that premium playing surface also makes them the most challenging type of pool table to move. Here's how professional pool table movers in Windsor approach slate table relocation safely and correctly.

Why Slate Tables Need Specialist Handling

A standard three-piece slate pool table has individual slate sections weighing 150–200 lbs each. The slate is brittle — it can crack if dropped, tipped, or placed incorrectly. Beyond the slate itself, the table's playing level depends on precise installation: even a few millimetres of unevenness will affect the roll of every shot.

The Disassembly Process for Slate Tables

  1. Remove pockets — leather or plastic pockets unscrew or unclip from the rail
  2. Remove rails — bolt-on rail sections are removed and wrapped in moving blankets
  3. Carefully remove the felt — either cut and discard (if replacing) or slowly unstaple (if reusing)
  4. Lift each slate section — three-person minimum; slates travel flat, never on edge
  5. Disassemble the frame and legs — bolt hardware is bagged and labelled

💡 Slates must always be transported flat — standing a slate on its edge during transit is one of the most common causes of cracking. Confirm this with your mover before booking.

Transport and Protection

Each slate section is wrapped in moving blankets and loaded flat in the truck. Rails and frame sections are also wrapped. Nothing is stacked on top of the slate during transit. The truck is loaded so slate sections are not shifting — straps and padding prevent any movement.

Reassembly and Leveling

At the destination, the frame is assembled first. Slate sections are then positioned, shimmed precisely, and the seams filled and smoothed. A level is used repeatedly to ensure the playing surface is perfectly flat. Only then is new or original felt installed, the rails bolted down, and pockets reattached.

How Long Does Slate Table Moving Take?

Full disassembly, transport, and reassembly for a three-piece slate table typically takes three to five hours for a local Windsor move. Budget more time for multi-floor moves or tight access situations.
