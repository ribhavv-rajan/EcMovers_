Office Moving Checklist
for Windsor Businesses

An office move without a checklist is a recipe for confusion, lost equipment, and unnecessary downtime. Whether you're relocating a 5-person office or a 50-person operation, this checklist gives you a structured approach to keeping your Windsor business move on track.

8–12 Weeks Before the Move

  • Confirm your new lease start date and access details
  • Book your moving company — get a written quote with your move date locked in
  • Design the floor plan for your new space and assign workstations
  • Appoint an internal move coordinator (single point of contact with movers)
  • Audit all IT equipment — servers, workstations, phones, printers
  • Notify clients and vendors of your upcoming address change

4–6 Weeks Before

  • Order packing supplies — boxes, tape, labels, markers
  • Assign packing responsibilities to each department or team
  • Book IT support for disconnection and reconnection at both locations
  • Arrange utilities transfer at both locations (internet especially)
  • Update your business address with Canada Post, CRA, and key vendors
  • Archive and purge old files — don't move paper you don't need

💡 The easiest time to cull old files, outdated equipment, and unwanted furniture is before a move — not after. Assign this task to each department 4 weeks out.

1–2 Weeks Before

  • Label all equipment with department name and destination workstation number
  • Use a colour-coded system — one colour per department or floor zone
  • Back up all company data — non-negotiable
  • Confirm mover arrival time, crew size, and truck details
  • Distribute the new office floor plan to all staff
  • Communicate move-day logistics to employees: what to pack personally, what the movers handle

Moving Day

  • Move coordinator on-site from start to finish
  • Post the floor plan at the new office entrance for movers
  • IT team on-site to begin reconnection as furniture arrives
  • Do a final walk-through of the old space before handing over keys
  • Check that all equipment is accounted for at the new location

Post-Move

  • Test all internet, phones, printers, and workstations
  • Update your Google Business profile and website with the new address
  • Notify any remaining vendors or clients who weren't contacted earlier
  • Schedule a team check-in one week after the move to flag any issues
